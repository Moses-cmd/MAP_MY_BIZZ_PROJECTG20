📘 Module 7: Digital Marketing for Business

Learn how to promote your business online — even with no budget or tech skills

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand what digital marketing is and why it matters
  • Use WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram to reach customers
  • Create simple, effective posts and messages
  • Turn followers into paying customers
  • Avoid common digital marketing mistakes

📱 1. What Is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing means using phones, apps, and the internet to promote your business.

You don’t need a website or money — just a smartphone and a plan.

  • Examples:
    • Sending a WhatsApp message: “New batch of muffins ready – R15 each!”
    • Posting a photo of your haircut on Instagram: “Open for bookings today!”
    • Sharing your location on Google Maps so customers can find you.

Why it works: More people use phones than ever before — meet them where they are!

💬 2. Marketing with WhatsApp & SMS

WhatsApp is one of the most powerful tools for small businesses.

  • Create a Broadcast List: Send updates to customers who agree to receive them.
  • Share Photos & Videos: Show your products in action.
  • Use Status Updates: Post daily specials, new stock, or promotions.
  • Reply Fast: Customers trust businesses that respond quickly.
Example Message:
Hi Nomsa! Just letting you know we have fresh vetkoek today 🫓🔥
Only R12 each or 5 for R50.
Delivery available in Soweto. Call or WhatsApp 072 XXX XXXX to order!

📘 3. Using Facebook & Instagram

Social media helps you reach new customers and build trust.

  • Set Up a Business Page: Use your business name, add photos, and write a short description.
  • Post Regularly: Even 1–2 times per week helps. Show:
    • New products
    • Happy customers
    • Behind-the-scenes (you baking, sewing, etc.)
  • Use Hashtags: #SowetoBaker #JHBStreetFood #SmallBusinessSA
  • Engage: Reply to comments and messages. Say “Thank you!”

Tip: Film short videos with your phone — people love real, honest content.

📍 4. Get Found on Google Maps

Many customers search “hair salon near me” or “mechanic in Cape Town” — make sure they find YOU.

  1. Open Google Maps on your phone or computer.
  2. Search for your business name and location.
  3. If it doesn’t exist, tap “Add your business”.
  4. Fill in:
    • Business name
    • Category (e.g., “Bakery”, “Barber Shop”)
    • Phone number
    • Photos of your shop or products
  5. Click “Submit” — it may take a few days to approve.

Pro Tip: Ask happy customers to leave a review: “Thank you for your support! If you enjoyed our service, please leave a review on Google 😊”

🎯 5. Turn Followers into Customers

More followers are great — but sales matter most. Try these tips:

  • Offer a Special Deal: “First 10 WhatsApp orders get 10% off!”
  • Use Urgency: “Only 5 left in stock!” or “Offer ends tonight!”
  • Share Proof: Post photos of deliveries, happy customers, or thank-you messages.
  • Make It Easy to Buy: Share your phone number, location, and payment details (EFT, Cash, Zapper).

Remember: One personal message can lead to 5 new customers through word-of-mouth.

🚫 6. Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Spamming: Don’t send too many messages — be helpful, not annoying.
  • Ignoring Messages: A reply builds trust. Even “Thanks, I’ll get back to you” works.
  • Poor Photos: Use natural light and clean backgrounds.
  • No Contact Info: Always include your number or WhatsApp link.

Golden Rule: Be real, be kind, be consistent.
